About Me

Welcome to my creative writing blog! I'm Ava Chu, a passionate writer, and a descendant of the Chu People in China. Although I'm a non-native English speaker, I have a deep love for writing in English. As an Esquire, I balance my legal career with my love for storytelling. Here, you'll find a collection of my short stories, flash fiction, and ongoing series. My journey as a writer has been fueled by my love for storytelling, and I'm excited to share my work with you.
